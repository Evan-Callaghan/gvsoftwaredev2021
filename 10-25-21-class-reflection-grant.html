Waterfall:

  • Waterfall project management is a traditional model for developing engineering systems. When applied to software development, specialized tasks completed in one phase need to be reviewed and verified before moving to the next phase. Some benifits of waterfall include, straightforward planning and designing, defined scope of work, defined team roles.
